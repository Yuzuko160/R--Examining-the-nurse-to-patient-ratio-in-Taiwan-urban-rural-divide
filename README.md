# Examining the Nurse-to-Patient Ratio in Taiwan’s Urban-Rural Divide

### *Using the onset, severity, and mitigation of COVID-19 as a reference*

---

## 📋 Project Overview
This project explores the disparities in nursing resources across Taiwan, specifically focusing on the **nurse-to-patient ratio**. By analyzing data from the three distinct phases of the COVID-19 pandemic, this research highlights how healthcare workloads shifted between urban centers and rural areas during a global crisis.

## 🔍 Key Research Areas
* **Temporal Analysis:** Comparing data across the onset, peak (severity), and mitigation stages of COVID-19.
* **Geographic Disparity:** Investigating the "Urban-Rural Divide" in Taiwan’s medical infrastructure.
* **Statistical Methodology:** Applying probability and statistical models to quantify healthcare pressure.
